## Source-Control Rules

<div class="water-vault-panel">

1. Treat local books as review sources, not redistributable vault content.
2. Extract only original summaries, source IDs, page references, and verified facts.
3. Keep copied prose out of vault notes unless the source is public-domain/open-licensed and redistribution is confirmed.
4. Recheck official legal/regulatory sources before relying on any dated value, PFAS limit, CT table, public-notification timing, or cross-connection rule.
5. Prefer official California DDW, EPA, eCFR, and adopted policy documents for final regulatory wording.

</div>

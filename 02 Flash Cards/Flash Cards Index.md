## Printable Rule

Final printable sheets should not be created until the related deck is validated. Draft packets are allowed if clearly marked as draft or partially verified.

Lab procedure, cross-connection, and distribution-source cards are usable for internal study but must remain caveated as operator-study summaries, not certified-lab SOPs, approved tester manuals, engineering specifications, or local program rules.

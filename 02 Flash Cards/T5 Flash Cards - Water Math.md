## Verification Notice

This deck is **partially verified**, not final-certified.

- Core formulas/constants: [[Water Operator Source Bibliography#CA-EXAM-CONVERSION-2026]]

Important study caveats:

- The official California 2026 Exam Conversion Sheet gives **1 MGD = 694.4 gpm**. Some cards use **approximately 694 gpm** for rounded mental math.
- `lb/day = mg/L × MGD × 8.34` gives active chemical need as pounds per day. Liquid commercial-solution feed requires percent strength and chemical weight or specific gravity.
- CT formula examples are math practice. Actual required CT must come from the correct official CT table for disinfectant, organism, pH, temperature, and log inactivation target.

### Card 8

Back: It converts mg/L and MGD into pounds per day.

Back: mg/L = lb/day ÷ (MGD × 8.34).

### Card 13

Back: Ask whether the number is reasonable.

### Card 15

Back: Estimation catches decimal and unit mistakes.

### Card 16

### Card 18

Back: Write the formula first, then substitute values.

### Card 19
Front: What does percent mean in calculations?
Back: Parts per hundred.

### Card 20
Front: How do you convert percent to decimal?
Back: Divide by 100.

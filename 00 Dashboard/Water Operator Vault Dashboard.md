## How It Works

<div class="water-vault-panel">

| Step | What to use |
|---|---|
| 1 | Print or review flash cards before a study session. |
| 2 | Take an on-screen practice exam. The quiz app saves answers in the browser on this computer. |
| 3 | Open the progress report to see strengths, weaknesses, and suggested study areas. |

</div>

<div class="water-vault-callout">
The progress report uses attempts saved by the on-screen quiz app in the same browser profile. If no attempts appear, take or restart one on-screen exam first.
</div>

> Original T5-style water math drills for [[T5 Study Hub]]. Work these closed-note first, then review [[T5 Formula Sheet]], [[Conversions Master Review]], [[Dimensional Analysis for Operators]], [[CT Calculations]], and [[T5 Practice Math Bank]].

## Core Formula Reminders

- **Chemical feed:** `lb/day = mg/L × MGD × 8.34`
- **Detention time:** `time = volume ÷ flow`
- **CT:** `CT = disinfectant residual × effective contact time`
- **Flow:** `Q = area × velocity`
- **Pressure/head:** `1 psi ≈ 2.31 ft of head`; `1 ft of head ≈ 0.433 psi`
- **Water weight:** `1 gal ≈ 8.34 lb`; `1 ft³ ≈ 7.48 gal`
- **1 MGD ≈ 694 gpm`

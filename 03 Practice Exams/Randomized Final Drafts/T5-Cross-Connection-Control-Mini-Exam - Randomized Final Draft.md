## Questions

1. What is a cross-connection? A) A filter-to-waste valve B) A chlorine sample point only C) A finished-water storage tank D) A physical or potential connection between potable water and a source of unknown or lesser quality
2. What is backflow? A) Filtered water entering a clearwell B) Normal finished-water flow C) Unwanted reverse flow into the potable water system D) Routine hydrant flushing
3. Backsiphonage is caused by: A) Higher downstream pressure B) Reduced supply pressure C) High fluoride residual D) Low hardness
4. Backpressure is caused by: A) Downstream pressure greater than supply pressure B) Reduced supply pressure only C) A sample tap D) Low turbidity
5. Which method provides the strongest physical separation? A) Plain hose bibb B) Unlisted adapter C) Air gap D) Single check valve
6. What does RP stand for? A) Reduced-pressure principle assembly B) Residual pressure pipe C) Reverse pump D) Routine pressure
7. What does DC stand for in this context? A) Direct current B) Distribution chlorine C) Daily calculation D) Double check valve assembly
8. A PVB protects against: A) Radionuclides only B) Backpressure only C) Backsiphonage only D) Pump cavitation
9. An AVB is generally not used under: A) Continuous pressure B) Point-of-use conditions C) Open discharge conditions D) Atmospheric conditions
10. A dual check is: A) A turbidity meter part B) Always the same as an RP assembly C) Not the same as a testable DC assembly D) The highest physical separation
11. What is containment protection? A) Protection only inside a single faucet B) A jar test method C) Protection at the service connection D) A chlorine demand test
12. What is isolation protection? A) Only a billing meter B) Only protection at the water source C) Only a tank screen D) Protection at or near the internal hazard
13. AWWA C510 covers: A) All California rules B) Double check valve backflow prevention assemblies C) All field-test procedures D) All wastewater lab methods
14. A complete DC assembly under AWWA C510 includes: A) Only a vacuum breaker B) Only an air gap C) One check valve and no test cocks D) Two independent check valves, two shutoff valves, and four test cocks
15. AWWA C510 is best used for: A) Replacing tester procedures B) Replacing local rules C) DC assembly construction/design study points D) Setting PFAS MCLs
16. Why must a testable assembly be accessible? A) It must be tested, maintained, repaired, or replaced B) It must be hidden C) It must be painted D) It must always be buried
17. Why does RP drainage matter? A) RP assemblies never discharge B) Drainage replaces testing C) The relief valve may discharge and needs a safe location D) Drainage proves pH compliance
18. What can an unprotected bypass do? A) Eliminate records B) Defeat the intended protection C) Replace the assembly D) Improve testing automatically
19. Who controls actual approved installation and testing requirements? A) Authority having jurisdiction and program rules B) Product catalog only C) A random online quiz D) Handwritten shortcut only
20. Which type protects against backsiphonage only and not backpressure? A) RP B) Air gap C) DC D) PVB
21. Which type is commonly associated with high-hazard protection where approved? A) Pipe coupling B) Hose adapter only C) Single check valve D) RP assembly
22. Which type is commonly limited to lower-hazard use where approved? A) Single check only B) Unprotected bypass C) Missing air gap D) DC assembly
23. What should be recorded for an assembly program? A) Customer name only B) Paint color only C) Weather only D) Type, size, model, serial number, location, test date, result, and tester information
24. What does a flow arrow show? A) Meter reading date B) Required direction of flow C) Pipe age D) Pump horsepower
25. Which source is still needed for detailed field-test procedures? A) Taste chart B) PFAS brochure C) USC Foundation Manual or accepted local tester reference D) Fluoride MCL table
26. Why is hazard level important? A) It replaces testing B) It only determines pipe color C) It determines the level of protection needed D) It removes local authority requirements
27. Which is true about AWWA C510? A) It is the USC field-test manual B) It is Title 22 C) It is a standard for DC assemblies, not a complete program manual D) It is a PFAS rule
28. Which item is usually part of a testable assembly? A) Fluoride probe only B) Test cocks C) Jar-test paddle D) Sample incubator
29. What should happen when local rules are more specific than a study note? A) Ignore them B) Follow the local rules C) Use oldest note only D) Use shortest answer
30. What is the best study caveat? A) They eliminate testing requirements B) Study aids do not replace approved program rules or tester procedures C) They replace all manuals D) They are official exam questions
